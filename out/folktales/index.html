ABOUT TAROK STORYTELLING

In Tarok culture, storytelling (called zuur) is a sacred tradition passed down by elders. Stories are told during festivals, around fires at night, and during important rites of passage. The Icir festival and Nang funeral celebrations are rich with oral tradition that carries the Tarok language forward through generations.
